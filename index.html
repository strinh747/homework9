About Me

Sandy Trinh, Ed.S., is a Nationally Certified School Psychologist, and is currently completing her pre-doctoral internship in clinical child psychology with a focus on autism spectrum disorder and neurodevelopmental disabilities at the University of Miami Mailman Center for Child Development. She is interested in technology and innovation aimed at improving access to intervention and education for those with developmental disabilities in low-resourced areas.
